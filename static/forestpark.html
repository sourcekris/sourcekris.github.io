Moving In & Infrastructure Checklist

1. Essentials Connection

Connect Electricity
Provider of choice (e.g., Red, OVO, Globird). Check for "Time of Use" tariffs.
Connect Gas
Often cheaper to bundle with electricity. Ensure heating is ready for winter.
Notify Sydney Water
Usually handled by conveyancer, but double check account setup.

2. High Speed NBN (FTTP)

Order 1000/50 Mbps Plan
Rec: Aussie Broadband or Superloop. Ensure activation date aligns with move-in.
Upgrade Router Hardware
Essential: You need a router with high WAN-to-LAN throughput (e.g., Ubiquiti Dream Machine, ASUS RT-AX86U) to actually see 1Gbps speeds.

3. Solar + Battery Project

Dependency: Roof work MUST happen before solar installation.
Step 1: Roof Restoration
Hire roofer for cleaning + tile repairs (per building report). Ensure structural integrity for panels.
Step 2: Get 3x Solar Quotes
Target: 6.6kW - 10kW PV + 10-13kWh Battery (e.g., Tesla Powerwall 2 or Sungrow).
Step 3: Installation
Coordinate ASAP after roof repairs.

4. Smart Home Cabling

Goal: Convert Coax to Ethernet & Centralize in Study Wardrobe.

Procure Hardware
- 10" Mini Rack (w/ fans for wardrobe)
- 12-24 Port Patch Panel
- Cat6 or Cat6a Cable (Solid Core)
- RJ45 Keystone Jacks & Wall Plates
Engage Registered Cabler
Legal Requirement: In-wall cabling MUST be done by a licensed pro.
Execute Re-Cabling
1. Pull old Coax, replace with Cat6.
2. Reroute 5x PoE Camera lines from Living -> Study.
3. Terminate all to Study Patch Panel.
